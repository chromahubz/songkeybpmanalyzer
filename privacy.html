Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Key Of Song is designed for on-device processing.

On-Device Processing

All audio analysis is performed locally in your browser on your device. We do not upload your files to our servers.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data or audio files. There are no accounts required and no tracking of uploaded content.

Cookies and Analytics

We aim to avoid invasive tracking. If we use any analytics or essential cookies, they are solely for improving reliability and performance, not for profiling users. We will update this policy if this changes.

Third-Party Services

The app may load libraries from CDNs for functionality. These providers may receive standard technical information (e.g., IP, user agent) to deliver assets. We do not share user data with third parties for advertising.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with an effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Key Of Song team via our GitHub repository issues.
